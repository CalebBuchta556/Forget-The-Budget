Welcome!

Forget The Budget was designed for people who want their budget out of sight, but not out of mind.

Forget The Budget uses your last months income, upcoming expenses, and a chosen savings rate to tell you exactly how much you can spend and still meet your savings goal. It is recommended to have at least a one month buffer of cash in your account to not only make budgeting easier, but for greater peace of mind!

The amount indicated above is intended to be spent on non-essential purchases such as dining out and entertainment.
